# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Projects (Excel & Power BI)

##Repository Overview
This repository contains **multiple end-to-end E-commerce Sales Analysis projects** developed using **Excel and Power BI**, designed to simulate real-world business and corporate analytics workflows.

Excel is implemented not only for data preparation but also for **data collection, structuring, and preliminary analysis**, with finalized datasets and KPIs visualized through **interactive Power BI dashboards** to deliver actionable business insights.

Each project focuses on transforming raw e-commerce sales data into **clear, decision-ready insights** covering revenue performance, product trends, and customer behavior.

---

##Objectives
- Collect, organize, and validate e-commerce sales data using Excel  
- Analyze sales performance and revenue trends across products and time periods  
- Track key business KPIs such as sales, growth, and category performance  
- Identify top-performing products and sales drivers  
- Visualize insights through interactive Power BI dashboards and reports  

---
> Each project folder includes Excel-based data collection and preparation files, along with Power BI dashboards and supporting documentation.

---

##Tools & Technologies Used
- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Data collection and structuring  
  - Data cleaning, validation, and transformation  
  - Pivot Tables, formulas, and KPI calculations  

- **Power BI**
  - Data modeling and relationships  
  - Interactive dashboards and visual analytics  
  - KPI tracking and trend visualization  

- **Presentation & Documentation**
  - PowerPoint / PDF for business reporting and insights communication  

---

##Key Analysis Areas
- E-commerce sales and revenue trend analysis  
- Product and category performance evaluation  
- Time-based analysis (monthly, quarterly, yearly)  
- KPI monitoring and comparative performance analysis  
- Insight generation to support business decision-making  

---

##Business Impact
The projects in this repository demonstrate how Excel-based data collection combined with Power BI visualization can:
- Improve sales performance monitoring and reporting  
- Enable faster and more accurate business insights  
- Support pricing, marketing, and inventory decisions  
- Enhance stakeholder communication through clear dashboards
